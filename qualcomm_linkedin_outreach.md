## What NOT to do

- Don't ask for a referral in the first message
- Don't send a wall of questions before they accept
- Don't ask "what specific questions did they ask you" — feels NDA-adjacent
- Don't send to 8 people in the same hour — LinkedIn flags as spam (space them across 1-2 days)
- Don't follow up if they don't respond within a week — move on

## What to do after responses

- Thank everyone who replies, even if "I don't know"
- Save useful intel back to `qualcomm_interview_intel.md`
- If anyone offers a referral, accept gratefully
- Post-screen, send a one-line thank-you update — builds real network for future
